Data Collection

Parliamentary Questions of Taiwan Legislative Yuan

The parliamentary questions (專案質詢) from the Website of Legislative Yuan (立法院) from 1994 to 2020, including categories of information with regards to the topic, keywords and the type. The dataset available upon request.

Central Bankers' Speeches

Extracted Central Bankers' Speeches on the Website of Bank for International Settlements (https://www.bis.org) from 1997 to 2020. The dataset available upon request.
